Item 2. Properties.
The Partnership is landlord only and does not participate in the operations of any of the properties discussed herein. All properties were paid for in cash, without any financing. The General Partners believe that the Partnership's properties are adequately insured.
Pursuant to the approval of the Special Master and the United States District Court, on August 7, 2000, an affiliated entity purchased all but two of the Partnership's properties. In January 2001, an affiliated party purchased one of the properties for a contract sales price of $275,000. In December 2001, the sole remaining property was sold to a third party for a contract sales price of $350,000.
